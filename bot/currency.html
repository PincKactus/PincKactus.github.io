Currency System

The bot supports five currencies you can earn, convert, give, and spend in its shop:

Key Name Emoji Rate to Celestine
celestine Celestine 🔆 1.0
lumina Lumina ✨ π
chronostone Chronostone ⏳ π × √2
obsidian Obsidian 🜨 π × √2 × e
abyssal Abyssal 🌌 π × √2 × e × φ

How Conversion Works

Internally, any amount is first converted into its “Celestine equivalent” via the rates above, then divided by the target currency’s rate. The command λconvert <amt> <from> <to> will prompt you to confirm before adjusting your balances.

Commands

  • λbalance – View your current wallet across all currencies.
  • λconvert <amt> <from> <to> – Convert between currencies.
  • λgive @user <amt> <currency> – Gift currency to someone else.
  • λmanifest <user> <amt> <currency> – (Admin) Add currency to a user.
  • λbanish <user> <amt> <currency> – (Admin) Remove currency from a user.
