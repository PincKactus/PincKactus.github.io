Currency System

The bot supports five currencies you can earn, convert, give, and spend in its shop. Exchange rates are given relative to 1 Celestine:

Name Symbol Exchange Rate
(to Celestine)
Celestine 🔆 1
Lumina ✨ π (≈ 3.14)
Chronostone ⏳ π√2 (≈ 4.44)
Obsidian 🜨 π√2 × e (≈ 12.07)
Abyssal 🌌 π√2 × e × φ (≈ 19.52)

How Conversion Works

Internally, amounts are converted into their Celestine equivalent using the radicals above, then re‐expressed in the target currency—always prompting you to confirm the result.

Commands

  • λbalance – View your wallet across all currencies.
  • λconvert <amt> <from> <to> – Convert between currencies.
  • λgive @user <amt> <currency> – Gift currency to someone else.
  • λmanifest <user> <amt> <currency> – (Admin) Add currency to a user.
  • λbanish <user> <amt> <currency> – (Admin) Remove currency from a user.
